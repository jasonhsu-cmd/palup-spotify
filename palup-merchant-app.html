How billing works

good to know · read once
What you’re charged for

You’re billed only when Aria does something for a customer — a message sent, a conversation handled, or content you approve.

  • Marketing emails & SMS sent
  • Live customer conversations handled
  • Images & video you approve
  • Each time you ask Aria to regenerate a visual
What happens if you reach your cap

Early warnings and daily forecasts keep you from getting here. But if you hit your hard cap and choose not to raise it:

Aria pauses proactive work
Campaigns, outreach, new content and proactive nudges stop — the spending you control pauses first.
Live customer chat keeps running
In a basic mode — answers from what Aria already learned about your store, plus a clean handoff to your team. At no cost to you.
Your customers never see any of this
No error, no “unavailable”, nobody cut off mid-purchase — and they’re never shown your billing status. Full Aria is back the moment you raise your cap or upgrade.
You stay in control: any extra is billed only up to the hard cap you set — never a dollar more.
